Secure Work From Home

New Normal or Not, Work From Home results in huge savings, but Security?

At SNS, we understand that work-from-home solution it not something that can be ordered from online. Solutions are specific to your environment. Do you have 100% SaaS applications, or hybrid SaaS & On-Prem or 100% on-prem only? From where, how and when your end-users access these applications?

Endpoint Detection & Response

Still relying only on Signature based AV & Anti-Spam?

Sophisticated attacks do not wait for the Security Programmers to develop a patch, update or signature to handle the imminent crisis. What you need is a Smart end-point solution that can dynamically understand and learn, and MOST importantly protect!

Security on Cloud

Moved to Cloud! Still Restless?

Moving to cloud could be just one part of your digital journey. However, Security is a Shared Responsibility as claimed by all of the Public Cloud providers. Here you need specific solutions that can protect your assets on cloud. SNS is here to help!

Virtual Desktops

Machine goes! And goes with the Data!

VDI or Virtual Desktop Infrastructure, provides greater control and security on your endpoint work environment. What applications to run, where to store and after a session ends, how to secure! These are the finer aspects of VDI.

Data Loss Prevention

Next time when your user uploads a file to cloud storage!

Endpoint DLP solutions ensure Corporate data is not leaked. Integration with O365 and Network DLP secures your Corporate data to a greater extent!
