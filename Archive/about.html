About Us

We are the warriors who fight on your behalf to protect your networks, data, applications and users. When others are struggling with Cyber Attacks, Ransomware Attacks, Phishing and many more, our Customers relax and conduct their business as usual.

We take the sting out of Ransomware and Cyber Attacks by protecting the integrity and confidentiality of your systems and ensuring that your business is not affected by any external or internal threats. When the market was only introduced to Antivirus,Secure Network Solutions (SNS) made it's way to Indian market with operations in Year 2000 introducing Firewalls, Intrusion Prevention System, Two-Factor Authentication and thus becoming pioneers in IT Security System Integration business.

Even today, SNS is synonymous to Watchguard among our Customers.

Our Mission is to provide the entire range of Network and Data Security Solutions and be recognized as the One-Stop-Place for any Security requirements.

Our Values are to achieve highest level of customer satisfaction, support standards and employee satisfaction.


Mr. Nand Kumar Mehta (N K Mehta)

SNS Founder,Chairman and Managing Director

Founder, Chairman and Managing Director of Secure Network Solutions, Mr. N.K. Mehta (BIT, Sindri & XLRI, Jamshedpur) has over 32 years of technical and industry experience. He was the AVP-IT of Gulf International Bank (GIB), Bahrain for 7-years and prior to that was working for Tata Motors for 7 years. He himself has directly deployed and managed large LAN/WAN, Infrastructure Security projects for networks spanning across UK, USA and the Middle East prior to establishing SNS along with technocrats of the industry.


Mr. Mehta is an active ISODA (Infotech Software Dealers Association) member and the current Chairman (2018-19), and has been awarded the "Most Impactful Tech Leader" award by "itVARnews" for his contributions to the IT industry.


He inspires and leads the highly qualified, industry trained and certified security specialists who manage large and complex IT Security projects of over 1000 customers across India and abroad
