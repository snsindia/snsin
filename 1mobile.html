Mobile Security Threat

Due to Covid , there was a rush to setup systems for Work-From-Home. Several organizations were not equipped with spare laptops to provide their employees. Few purchased new, few opted for rental and rest allowed personal laptops/desktops to be used.

We were flooded with calls to setup security for such WFH laptops/desktops and to ensure secure connectivity to On-Premise or On-Cloud servers.

But we felt there was a major gap! While organizations provided laptop to their employees and ensured the security of such devices , no one was talking about Mobile security !
